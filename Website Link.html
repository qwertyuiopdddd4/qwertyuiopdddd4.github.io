Fashion

Fashion now has become an inherent part of people’s lives, especially in urban settings. One’s fashion sense may be expressed in numerous ways, such as by donning unique clothing, wearing accessories that carry certain identities.

Festivals

Festivals play an important role in preserving cultural heritage, celebrating tradition, and building community. They provide a platform for people to come together and celebrate their shared values, beliefs, and customs.

T‘Music as culture’ examines the connections that ethnomusicologists make between music and culture. Culture, in an ethnomusicologist sense, refers to all forms of human knowledge, creativity, and values and identites.
